The digital currency for sustainable farming

Too often, farmers aren’t treated fairly, despite how hard they work to grow the crops that feed all of us. Despite this, farmers from all parts of the world are working hard to grow sustainably. 

They are improving farm practices, applying water conservation techniques, managing nutrients and pesticides efficiently, reducing harmful crop residues, and ensuring farm workers’ health & safety. 

We want to recognise and reward these farmers, particularly those who are trying to implement a sustainability standard.

FarmChange is for farmers everywhere

From smallhold farmers in Asia, to tech-savvy farmers in the Midwest.  Whether you are just starting out with sustainable growing practices, or already compliant with a third-party standard, you can start earning FarmChange.

Spending your FarmChange

FarmChange can be redeemed for deals and discounts at a number of Participating Partners.

FarmChange is created by Better Grain

for the past 8 years, we’ve been working with thousands of farmers around the world on improving crop yields and livelihoods through sustainable agriculture.

Earning your FarmChange

Earning FarmChange is easy.  Farmers that are on the path to sustainable growing can start earning FarmChange right away.  Just answer a few questions about what you grow and the sustainability standards you’re working towards, and start earning FarmChange.
